The following items are explicitly out of scope for Project Chimera.
Agents must not attempt to implement or extend into these areas.

---

## Behavioral Non-Goals

- Real-time human impersonation
- Deceptive identity masking
- Emotional manipulation of users
- Autonomous political persuasion

---

## Content Non-Goals

- Medical advice generation
- Legal advice generation
- Financial investment advice
- Misinformation amplification

---

## System Non-Goals

- Prompt-only automation without governance
- Self-modifying agent objectives
- Unbounded autonomous decision-making
- Hidden or unaudited agent actions

---

## Development Non-Goals

- Rapid prototyping without specifications
- Vibe coding or exploratory agent behavior
- Feature development without traceability

Anything listed here must not be implemented,
even if technically feasible.
